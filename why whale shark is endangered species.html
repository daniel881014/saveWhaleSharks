why whale shark is endangered species

There are many fact that why the whale shark in endanger species, here is some of the reason that whale sharks is endangered species is due to human irresponsible commercial fishing and tourism, also the fin and the meat can be use in traditional Chinese medicine.

The other reason is due to the climate change whale shark's habitats is being damaged.


What can we do to protect.

You don't have to be a biology scientist to save the whale sharks, everyone is able to save it by doing the following:

First of all, is not to buy whale shark part's related products, the sales relect to the producer that it is not profitable, therefore they would not hunt whale shark.

Second, when going to the tour make sure to find a responsible tourist guide, that when enteracting with whale shark or other wild life, put animal's health and safty first.

Third, research and understaning the animal, also if possible donate to the reliable organization. Such as WWF

Lasty, teach other about whale shark and encourage them to also save this beautiful creature. Education is the key to everything, saving amimal is not exception.
