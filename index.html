Diet & Reproduction

Whale shark is a filter feeder it is one of the only three filter feeder shark. Filter feeder is a type of animal that feed themself by swiming pass water and use specialized filter structure to eat. This filter feeder styles makes whale shark to swimming with open mouth. Whale shark eat different variety of eat creature, such as plankton, krill,fish eggs and some time small sea life, such as small squid or fish.

A female whale shark can give bith up to 300 pups, however, not all the pups are able to survived to maturity, most of time only 1 to 10 are able to reach maturity.
